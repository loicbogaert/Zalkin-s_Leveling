First of all, the bandits quest will give you rewards based on which bandit you chose, so you have to know which bandit you want to help, or if you want to kill all bandits.


Usually, you will choose either to help Alira, or to kill all bandits.

If you are following a guide, try to find out which one the guide tells you to pick.

If not, know that Help Alira or Kill All Bandits are probably the two best options.

If you want to change later on, you will be able change your bandit bonus with a vendor recipe.


Kill All Bandits : +2 skill points Kill the three bandits and talk to Eramir in town


Help Alira : 5 mana regen / +20% to global critical strike multiplier / +15% to all Elemental resistances Kill Kraityn and Oak - Help Alira (talk to her at the end)


Help Kraityn : 6% attack and cast speed / 3% chance to dodge attacks / 6% increased movement speed Kill Alira and Oak - Help Kraityn (talk to him at the end)


Help Oak : 1% of life regenerated per second / 2%additionnal physical damage reduction / 20% increased physical damage Kill Kraityn and Alira - Help Oak (talk to him at the end)
